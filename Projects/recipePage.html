Fayarita Spagetti

Here's how to make this recipe

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • fish
  • Vegetables
  • V.Oil
  • Water
  • seasoning

Instructions

  1. Per Boil the Spagetti for 6 minutes and drain the water in a sieve
  2. Add V.oil in a dry pot on the fire
  3. Put the vegetables into the pot.
  4. Add the seasoning and stir
  5. Add little water then add the spagetti. Stir allow to simmer for two minutes
